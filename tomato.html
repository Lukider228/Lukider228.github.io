Agridera Fresh Market Kiwi Hybrids

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of kiwi seeds and cultivars. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world. In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant cultivars adapted to different environmental conditions and markets globally.

Currently, emphasis is placed on combining resistance to PSA (Pseudomonas syringae pv. actinidiae) and Kiwi Berry Root Rot with other soil-borne tolerances into superior kiwi breeding lines and hybrids. Moreover, comprehensive efforts are being undertaken to develop varieties with enhanced resilience to emerging viral and bacterial threats affecting vine health.

In terms of horticultural traits, breeding is focused on harvest earliness, optimized fruit size, flesh firmness, vibrant internal color (Gold, Green, and Red variations), balanced sugar-to-acid flavor profiles, skin smoothness, extended shelf life, and consistent fruit set under varying temperature fluctuations. Combinations of premium quality features and robust disease resistance are sought across a variety of types, specifically focusing on both smooth-skinned and traditional fuzzy varieties.

Agridera Kiwi breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: High-Tech Greenhouse Cultivation, Intensive Orchard Trellis Systems, and Climate-Resilient Open-Field Plantations.

Greenhouse Kiwi Breeding Program

This breeding program is aimed at the Spanish and Turkish markets. It focuses on both single-fruit and cluster-harvest varieties, characterized by a consistent oval-to-round fruit shape. Key breeding goals include robust resistance to PSA (Pseudomonas syringae pv. actinidiae), Kiwi Berry Root Rot, and Soil-borne Nematodes.Some premium hybrids may include additional resistances, such as tolerances to specific fungal pathogens like Botrytis and Sclerotinia.

Orchard-Intensive Indeterminate Kiwi

This breeding segment is designed for the Brazilian, Moroccan, and Turkish markets. The primary focus is on developing large-caliber fruits (120 grams plus) with exceptional flesh density. Priorities include integrated resistance to PSA, Phytophthora, and Root-knot Nematodes. In the near future, enhanced tolerance to Armillaria Root Rot will be included as a standard resistance trait in our commercial cultivars.

Open-Field Bush-Type Kiwi Varieties

This breeding program is dedicated to the Mediterranean markets. We develop both traditional fuzzy and smooth-skinned elongated shapes, ranging from standard sizes (80–100 grams) to "Jumbo" beef-style sizes (minimum 110 grams up to 150 grams and more). We combine resistance to PSA and Nematodesas core traits in our hybrids. Additionally, selected hybrids may feature further protections, such as resistance to Mildew (Oidium) and environmental stress tolerances for open-air cultivation.

Agridera operates through international and local distributers around the world.
